BRUNDAVANAM
We started our journey in 2015 , as a cow-based farming station. BRUNDAVANAM has many geographical significances. Its on the foot of Eastern ghats, with abundant water resources. And also, very convenient for rearing cows with lot of scope for grazing. The most important aspect is ,its very close to forest where lot of herbs are available which are essential in making panchagavya medicines.
Cow Based Farming
First we have developed a Gouber Gas plant with a dual purpose of getting clean fuel for cooking and CHEMICAL FREE manure for the soil. We are committed for training the local farmers to create awareness on Cow based farming and its effects on environment and society.
Gopooja
According to Indian culture, Cows are considered to be holy, and even to give respect, we call them Gowmaata, which means a cow is like a mother.

Free-Grazing

We send cows for grazing every day. We believe cows should be left freely.The animals raised this way are more healthy with less fat in their tissues.

Colostrum feeding

We reared the calves with open heart and always grow them by giving due share of milk for them. That helps the calves to fight against bacterial and viral diseases and helps in the development of the calves.

Organic Animal Feed

We have grown our own Organic animal feed with no added synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilisers.Due to this, there is no chance of chemical residues in the Panchgavya products we make.
